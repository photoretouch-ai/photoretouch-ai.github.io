PhotoUp Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

Welcome to PhotoUp. This Privacy Policy explains how InflyJoy Mobile (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. PhotoUp is an AI-powered photo editing application providing smart features such as AI Enhance, Background Remove, Color Correction, Retouch, and other intelligent image processing tools to help you achieve professional results with minimal effort. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly and transparently.

1. Data Controller and Representative

InflyJoy Mobile and its subsidiaries (“PhotoUp”, “we”, or “our”) act as the data controller. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws to ensure lawful handling of your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. What Kind of Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personally identifiable information (“Personal Information”):

  • Name, email address, phone number, or social media profile (e.g., Facebook, Twitter) used to log into PhotoUp
  • Photos and metadata you upload or edit using the app
  • Location data (e.g., from GPS, Wi-Fi, or IP address)
  • Technical log data (device type, OS version, IP address, app version, screen resolution, etc.)
  • Usage data related to app features, tools used, and export behavior

2.2 Non-Personal Information

This includes aggregated, anonymized, or technical information that cannot be used to identify you directly, such as device language, timezone, app list, or hardware specifications.

3. How We Use Collected Information

  • To operate and improve our app and features (e.g., AI Enhance, Remove, Filters)
  • To provide personalized user experiences and UI recommendations
  • To analyze usage patterns for product development
  • To deliver contextual advertisements and promotions
  • To prevent fraud, enforce terms, and ensure data security
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and technical support

We may share data with third-party service providers (such as analytics vendors or cloud hosting providers) only as needed to deliver our services securely and legally, and only under binding privacy and security agreements.

4. How Your Information May Be Disclosed

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or government requests
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities
  • To affiliates and partners involved in operating or supporting the app
  • In connection with business transfers or acquisitions

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may include links to third-party websites or display content from external sources. These third parties operate independently and are responsible for their own privacy practices. We encourage you to read their policies before engaging with their services.

6. Account and Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and safeguards to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure. Please protect your account credentials and avoid sharing your password with others.

7. International Data Transfers

We may store or process your information outside of your home country, including in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws. When doing so, we ensure that adequate protection measures are in place, such as standard contractual clauses (SCCs) or other lawful mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also object to certain uses of your data or withdraw consent at any time. Please contact us at photoup.feedback@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

10. Children’s Privacy

PhotoUp does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately. Please contact us if you believe this has occurred.

11. Sensitive Information

Please do not submit sensitive personal data (such as racial/ethnic origin, religious beliefs, or health status) through the app. We do not collect or process such data intentionally.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of material changes and update the "Effective Date" above accordingly. Continued use of the app signifies acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at photoup.feedback@gmail.com.
